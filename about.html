About CCCSP

The Cebu City College Scholarship Program supports deserving Cebu City students through tuition assistance and allowances.

Apply now Check status

What we provide

Key benefits and support options for qualified scholars.

Private schools

Up to ₱10,000 tuition assistance per semester.

Public schools

₱2,500 allowance for school supplies per semester.

Mountain barangays

Additional ₱5,000 transportation allowance for qualified students.

Our mission

Make quality education more accessible to Cebu City residents by simplifying applications, improving transparency, and supporting students through their academic journey.
