Quick checklist

Use this before you start your application.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Cebu City
  • Family income ≤ ₱400,000/year
  • Enrolled (or enrolling) in an accredited school

Documents

  • Proof of residency (ID / barangay certificate)
  • Proof of income
  • Enrollment proof / report card
  • Clear photos or scans
Tip: After you submit, save your control number. You’ll need it to check status.

Frequently asked questions

Short answers to common questions.

How do I apply?
Go to the Application page, complete the form, then upload your documents.
How do I check my application status?
Use your control number on the Status page. Keep it saved after you submit.
What if my family income is above ₱400,000?
You can still submit an application, but it may require additional manual review before a decision is made.
What documents do I need to prepare?
Common requirements include proof of residency, proof of income, and school/enrollment documents.
