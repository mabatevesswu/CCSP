FAQs

Where do I see my control number?
After you submit your application, you’ll receive a control number. Save it to check your status later.
Can I edit my application after submitting?
If you need to correct something, contact support and provide your control number for assistance.
I’m missing a document—should I still apply?
It’s best to submit complete requirements. If you must submit now, be ready to provide follow-up documents when requested.
