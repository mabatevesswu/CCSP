Scholarship Journey

Follow these steps from submission to awarding.

  1. 01

    Online registration

    Fill out the application form and upload scanned copies of your Voter's Certificate and Report Card.

  2. 02

    Document verification

    The CCCSP board reviews eligibility based on Cebu City residency and family income limits.

  3. 03

    Qualifying exam

    Qualified applicants receive a schedule for the career-pathing examination at the DepEd Division Office.

  4. 04

    Approval & payout

    Once approved, your name is included in the master list for the City Treasurer's Office to process your allowance.
